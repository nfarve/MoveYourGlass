Get Up Move More

Move Your Glass is a behavior and activity monitoring application. The application keeps track of physical activities the wearer engages in. Users are served suggestions at critical moments encouraging an activity or behavior change. A dashboard system will also give a summary of the users activities. The objective of the application is to, over time, enforce better lifestyle choices that would lead to more physical activity and social interactions.

Want it? Here's how to get it.

To get Move Your Glass on your google glass device:

  1. Download from the glass store or here.
  2. Register your device here using the id number given when you launch Move Your Glass.
